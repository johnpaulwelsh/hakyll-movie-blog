Swiss Army Man

Watched on March 13, 2026

Given this and EEAAO, it looks like the Daniels’ M.O. is to take a high-concept idea and take it as far as the script will possibly allow it. For this movie, shockingly, it does not overstay the gimmick. I laughed basically every time the corpse farted. And the editing for the quick montage scenes is top notch.

I particularly liked how Hank was explaining aspects of society and humanity, it reminded me of how I talk to my kids when they keep asking questions.

The introspective parts of the story, especially near the end, kind of fall flat for me, but I don’t know how it could have been improved. This was never going to be a 10/10 movie, but I think it basically achieves everything it set out to.

This is the most “film festival” thing I’ve ever seen, it just begs for a big blind audience reacting to it in a theater.

8/10
