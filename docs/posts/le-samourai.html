Le Samouraï

Watched on June 3, 2024

It’s a cool movie. It’s trying to be cool, and it succeeds, but at first I thought it wasn’t as cool as it was trying to be. Up until the sequence in the police station I thought it was just okay. But something about the movement through those rooms and the camera work and the editing made me like it a lot more. Then it just got better from there.

I do think the title is trying too hard. Maybe I don’t know enough about samurai tropes but Jef doesn’t really embody the samurai way to the degree I expected from the title. It’s not like Ghost Dog where he explicitly reads Japanese stuff. You could have easily called this movie The Hit Man and it would be just as good.

8/10
