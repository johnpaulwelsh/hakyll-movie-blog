Curtain Call

Watched on April 24, 2026

It tries to be a cute movie about a cute couple and a cute ghost dead couple, but the living couple is really boring, the ghost couple isn’t as funny as they could be, and the ordering and pacing of the scenes is really strange. The most interesting part was seeing Michael Caine and Maggie Smith at almost the same age, but seeming to be totally different ages just based on their level of wrinkles.

4/10
