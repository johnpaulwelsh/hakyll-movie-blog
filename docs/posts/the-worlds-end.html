The World's End

Watched on April 23, 2022

Just a grand old time. Few movies pull a plot 180 as well as this one does. Maybe overall the slow realization in Shaun of the Dead is more thematically rich, but you can’t beat the sudden bathroom brawl in The World’s End for sheer WTF-ery. The fight scenes in this movie are really interesting too, it seems like the camera is doing tricks to make the fights look more impactful and cool, but I can’t place what those tricks actually are. It’s all very dynamic and slick.

10/10
