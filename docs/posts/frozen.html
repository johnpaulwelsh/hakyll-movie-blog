Frozen

Watched on March 2, 2026

It’s enjoyable, but this time I realized how uneven the movie is. “For the First Time in Forever (Reprise)” is a really shoehorned-in musical number, it makes no sense for that to be a song. “Fixer Upper” completely halts the momentum and stakes of the story to have an annoying joke song that should be 30 mins earlier in the movie, tonally-wise. Olaf is on his own actually hilarious, but his reason for existing is hardly ever addressed and he kind of just exists. Hans’ turn to evil is very abrupt, like the movie really convinces you that he’s a good guy who’s taking care of the kingdom while Elsa and Anna are away, but then all of a sudden he’s a ruthless guy.

“Let It Go” is kind of weak as a song set to visuals, it works better as a musical track on its own.

There are a lot of songs early in this movie, like four or five in the first 30 minutes it feels like.

7/10
