Projekt (The Design)

Watched on April 22, 2026

Becomes more interesting and funnier as it goes, to my great surprise. The message about conformity is loud and clear, but it comes so abruptly that the impact is memorable.

I like the way a human hand looks in stop-motion, it’s not like time-lapse footage, it’s something more interesting and I can’t explain how.

7/10
