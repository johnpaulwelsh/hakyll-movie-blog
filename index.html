Welcome to my movie review blog!

This mostly will be a spot where I put short (maybe medium-length) reviews of all the movies I see, in roughly the order I see them. I might also sprinkle in some longer articles centering on some broader film-related thoughts.

These will not be fully qualified reviews so posts will make the most sense if you have seen the movie. Also, spoiler warnings apply to pretty much all posts.

I have an account on icheckmovies.com, where you can find the running list of every movie I have watched. I originally kept this in an Excel/Google Sheets document, but decided that was too cumbersome so I migrated that data to a profile on this site. Click here to see my profile!
