----
title: Zaniklý svět rukavic (The Vanished World of Gloves)
published: 2026-04-22
----

One of those shorts where you think to yourself, somebody actually sat down and made this whole thing? Especially because of how painstaking it must have been to animate it. But it does kind of feel like there's a point to it. There's something icky about almost all of the glove scenes except the first one, and it feels like each of them have something to say about the depravity of man. The construction worker at the end seems to be covering up the source of the glove films because he's disgusted. It's almost like throwing the Jumanji board game into the ocean so it can't harm anybody else.

8/10
