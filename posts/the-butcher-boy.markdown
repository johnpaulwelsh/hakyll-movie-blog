title: The Butcher Boy
published: 2026-04-22
----

It's cute, but it feels pretty scattershot. You can hardly call this The Butcher Boy because it's only about 70% about the boy, and he does butchery for about 5% of the runtime. There are parts which made me laugh (the dinner table at the girls' school, the molasses gag, the teacher with the gun) but others which felt very juvenile and there just for the fun of the actors instead of for actual comedic effect (the flour fight mostly).

It's weird to see a film with Buster Keaton in it but not necessarily starring him. Buster steals the show every time he's on screen though, that man can fall and get injured better than anybody else in this cast. I should really see a proper Fatty Arbuckle short to give him a full chance.

6/10
