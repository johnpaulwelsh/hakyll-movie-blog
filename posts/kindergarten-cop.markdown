title: Kindergarten Cop
published: 2026-04-11
----

The minute this movue started I knew I would like it, because that mall with the fountain in it just oozed 1990s in a way I haven't seen it captured in a long time.

Pamela Reed is so dang funny. Penelope Ann Miller is drop-dead gorgeous and looks uncannily like _Mulholland Dr._-era Naomi Watts, even down to the teeth.

The kids are unbelievably cute and funny, this would have been a 7/10 movie just for them. When the fat kid drops the food out of his mouth I felt that as a parent so hard.

There's definitely something interesting about a movie which is so steeped in the idea of broken families and divorce in the US, but which doesn't go explicit about any of the subtext about what that does to the personality of a kid or a parent. They could have easily had Cullen and his weird mom have an argument about "why was dad never around for me? That's why I am this way" and it would have cheapened the movie a lot, but they were thankfully smart enough to let the characters' brokenness speak for itself.

8/10
