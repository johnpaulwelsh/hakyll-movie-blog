----
title: Legally Blonde
published: 2026-03-27
----

It's cute and fun! Reese Witherspoon has the most infectious smile ever. The cast is surprisingly stacked. The fact that the movie begins and ends with "Perfect Day" by Hoku is just the most early-2000s thing ever.

Some parts felt a little shabby, like the blocking, editing, and writing of the almost-goodbye scene between Elle and the nails woman, there was a lot of dead air and awkward cuts. Also you never actually see Elle prove that she's smart enough to get into Harvard Law, unlike in _Clueless_ where it's obvious that Cher can kick herself into high gear when she's motivated, it's just that she cares about shallow things the most.

7/10
