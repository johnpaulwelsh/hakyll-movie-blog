Totally captivating. I'm kind of annoyed how every time the setting changes I get sucked right back in, even though I don't know anything about this new story and I know it's gonna last only like 25 minutes. The fact that in the credits each scenario is given a label based on the five senses (plus consciousness) is so intriguing.

The theater experience was phenomenal, the sound especially was overwhelming and immersive. The soundtrack for this movie carries a lot of weight.

What's it about you ask? I'm gonna say, when dreams are prohibited the only way you can express memories and feelings is through the closest medium there is, which is film. Each segment is clearly indebted to a style of film and expresses some kind of humanity.

9/10
