Notorious

Watched on December 20, 2021

Pretty much perfect. The drama, thrills, romance, and especially the performances are magnificent. The key and teacup are both instantly memorable objects, I can totally understand why they chose those scenes as a model for the DVD and Blu-Ray cases. I much prefer this kind of Hitchcock (along with Rebecca and Strangers on a Train) compared to something like Psycho.

10/10
