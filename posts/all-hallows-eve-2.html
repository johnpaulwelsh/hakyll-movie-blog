All Hallows Eve 2

Watched on November 3, 2020

Some shorts were fun but dumb, some were just good, and some were pretty stupid. But none of them felt like a waste of time, which is a plus.

This has the lamest framing device for a movie I’ve ever seen. It makes the first All Hallows Eve look like a masterpiece in comparison.
