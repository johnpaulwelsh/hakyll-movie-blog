A very shaggy script with some really unfunny moments (mostly the endless parade of Arthur being drunk). But I'll be damned if I say I didn't like it. Linda and Hobson being cute, separately or together, is enough for me to give this a 6/10. The musical score is something I wish we still had, lame yacht rock written by a master. It think this movie would work much less if they hadn't gone for the move of "everybody is insane." Especially Susan, the fact that she's crazy is a crucial tonal decision.

7/10
