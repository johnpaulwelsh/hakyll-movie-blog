A bona-fide classic. It becomes a bit less perfect as it gets past the halfway point, but basically every joke lands and there are too many iconic moments to mention.

The fact that this is so relatable to me in 2026 when it's commenting on office culture from the late 90s, is really impressive. This could have aged very poorly (like a lot of mid 00s comedies did), but I think this one still has that special sauce.

9/10
