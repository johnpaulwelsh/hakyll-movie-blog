title: Savage Island
published: 2026-05-29
----

Not the best so-bad-it's-good movie I've seen (_The Room_ can't be beat), and also not the worst movie on its own right. I kind of actually loved the shot-on-video blotchiness of the night scenes, unironically. To my delight it doesn't grind to a boring halt as it goes, some of the best parts are close to the end of the movie. Steven's character spends an awful lot of time silently crouching in the woods and watching bad things happen without trying to stop them. The sister of the savage family clearly wants to be in an incest movie but much to her disappointment she isn't. The violence is all offscreen to a surprising degree, maybe because the budget is so low.

2/10
